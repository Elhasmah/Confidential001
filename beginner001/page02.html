Nigerian Egg Roll

Introduction

Nigerian egg roll is a popular street food snack enjoyed throughout Nigeria. These delicious treats consist of hard-boiled eggs wrapped in a slightly sweet and fluffy dough, then deep-fried until golden brown. Similar to scotch eggs, but with a unique twist, Nigerian egg rolls have a soft savory interior complemented by a crispy outer shell. They're perfect for breakfast, snacks, or even as a quick meal on the go. Whether you're new to Nigerian cuisine or looking to recreate a nostalgic favorite, this egg roll recipe is easy to make and sure to delight! learn how to make delicious Nigerian egg roll with this easy-to-follow recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 4 eggs (boiled)
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. Add the melted butter and milk, mix untill you hvae a smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into four portions, flatten each piece and wrap it around a boiled egg.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan, and fry egg rolls until golden brown
  5. Remove and drain on a paper towel before serving. Enjoy!

Tips and Tricks

Ensure the oil is hot enough to prevent the dough from absorbing too much oil. You can test this by dropping a small piece of dough in the oil; if it sizzles, then the oil is ready.
